## Overview

This index catalogues all legal and compliance template documents maintained by the MediFleet Hospital Management System for use by hospital staff in Kenya. All templates are Kenya-jurisdiction documents and must be completed, signed, and retained in accordance with the laws referenced herein.

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Select the correct template** from the catalogue below based on the situation.
2. **Print on hospital letterhead** unless the template already contains a header block.
3. **Complete all `[FILL IN]` placeholders** before presenting the document to any patient, guardian, or third party. Never present a document with unfilled placeholders.
4. **Read the document aloud or explain it** to the patient/subject in a language they understand before requesting a signature.
5. **Obtain original wet signatures** for all consent, authorization, and agreement documents. Digital signatures are acceptable only where the MediFleet system provides a verified digital signature workflow.
6. **Scan and upload** the completed document to the patient's record in MediFleet immediately after signing.
7. **Retain the original paper copy** in the patient's physical file.
8. **Observe retention periods** strictly. Documents must not be destroyed before the minimum retention period has elapsed.
