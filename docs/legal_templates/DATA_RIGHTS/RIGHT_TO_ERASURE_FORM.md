## IMPORTANT NOTICE — MEDICAL RECORDS RETENTION

Under the **Kenya Health Act, 2017**, medical facilities are legally required to retain patient health records for a minimum of **seven (7) years** from the date of the last clinical entry.

**What this means for your request:**

When we receive a valid erasure request, we will **pseudonymise** your personal identifying information rather than permanently delete your records. This means:

- Your **name, phone number, email address, physical address, and ID number** will be replaced with anonymised placeholders.
- Your **clinical records** (diagnoses, lab results, treatment history, billing) will be retained for the legally mandated 7-year period with the identifying information removed.
- You will **no longer be able to log into the Patient Portal**, as your identifying information will be removed.
- After the 7-year retention period expires, your records will be permanently deleted.

If you have questions about this process, please speak to the hospital Data Protection Officer before submitting this form.
