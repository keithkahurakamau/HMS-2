## What "Restriction" Means

When processing is restricted, we may **store** your personal data but may only otherwise process it:
- With your consent
- For the establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims
- For the protection of the rights of another person
- For reasons of important public interest

Restriction does **not** mean deletion of your records.
