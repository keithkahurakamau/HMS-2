Pursuant to Section 30 of the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, the processing and sharing of personal data requires the informed, specific, and voluntary consent of the data subject. This form records your consent to the sharing of your personal health information with the third party specified below. You are not obliged to give this consent, and withholding it will not affect the quality of clinical care you receive except where the sharing is necessary for a referral or insurance claim you have requested.

## PART F — YOUR RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT

You may withdraw this consent at any time by completing a Consent Withdrawal Form (available from the Patient Records Office or from the MediFleet Patient Portal). Withdrawal of consent will take effect from the date the hospital receives your written withdrawal notice.

**Important:** Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of any sharing that took place before withdrawal. If data has already been transmitted to the recipient, the hospital will notify the recipient of the withdrawal, but cannot retrieve data already received by that party.
