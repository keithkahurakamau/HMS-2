## PART G — RIGHT TO WITHDRAW

You may withdraw your participation at any time, without giving any reason, and without any consequence to your ongoing medical care at this facility. To withdraw, notify the Principal Investigator or any member of the research team verbally or in writing.

**Important:** Data collected before the date of your withdrawal may be retained and used by the research team unless you specifically request otherwise in writing. Withdrawal of consent after data has been included in a published study may not be possible where the data is irretrievably anonymised.
