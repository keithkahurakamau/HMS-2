## LEGAL BASIS

Pursuant to Section 30(4) of the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, a data subject has the right to withdraw consent to the processing of their personal data at any time. Where consent is the basis for medical treatment, a patient may also withdraw treatment consent at any time in accordance with the common law doctrine of informed consent. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of any processing or treatment that took place before the withdrawal was received.
