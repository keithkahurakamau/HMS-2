## 2. WHAT PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU

We collect and process the following categories of personal data relating to staff:

**Identity and Contact Information:**
- Full name, date of birth, gender, and nationality
- National Identity Card number or Passport number
- Physical and postal address
- Personal telephone number(s) and personal email address (for emergency contact)
- Next of kin details (name, relationship, contact number)
- Passport-size photograph (for ID badge and personnel file)

**Employment Records:**
- Application form, curriculum vitae, and supporting documents
- Employment contract, job title, grade, and department
- Professional registration numbers (e.g., Kenya Medical Practitioners Board, Nursing Council of Kenya)
- Qualifications, certificates, and training records
- Performance appraisal and assessment records
- Disciplinary and grievance records
- Attendance, leave, and absence records (including sick leave and medical certification)
- References and pre-employment verification results (criminal background check, professional reference)

**Payroll and Financial Records:**
- Salary grade, allowances, and deductions
- Bank account details for salary payment
- NSSF and NHIF membership numbers
- KRA Personal Identification Number (PIN) for tax compliance
- Pension scheme membership details (where applicable)
- Expense claims and reimbursement records

**Occupational Health and Medical Records (where applicable):**
- Pre-employment medical examination results
- Vaccination records (e.g., Hepatitis B) required for clinical roles
- Work-related injury or illness records
- Occupational health referral and assessment records
- Fitness-for-duty assessments

**System Access Records:**
- MediFleet system username and access level (role-based)
- Audit logs of system access and actions (linked to your individual user account)
- Password reset history (password hash only — plain-text passwords are never stored)
- Login timestamps and session records

**CCTV and Physical Access Records:**
- CCTV footage captured on hospital premises during working hours and access-controlled areas
- Electronic access card entry/exit logs for restricted areas

## 6. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER KDPA 2019

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

- **Right to be informed** (S.25): This notice satisfies this requirement.
- **Right of access** (S.26): You may request a copy of the personal data held about you. Submit your request in writing to the Data Protection Officer.
- **Right to rectification** (S.27): You may request correction of inaccurate personal data. Contact HR.
- **Right to data portability** (S.28): Where technically feasible, you may request your data in a portable format.
- **Right to restriction** (S.34): You may request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
- **Right to object** (S.36): You may object to processing based on legitimate interests, subject to the lawfulness of the processing.

**Note:** Some rights are subject to limitations where processing is required by law (e.g., payroll tax records, audit logs required by KDPA 2019).

To exercise any of these rights, contact the **Data Protection Officer** at the details above. We will respond within **21 days** of receiving a complete request.
