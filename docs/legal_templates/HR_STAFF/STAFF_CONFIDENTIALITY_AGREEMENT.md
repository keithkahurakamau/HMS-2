## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Confidential Information"** means all patient personal data, clinical records, financial information, staff personal data, business processes, software systems (including MediFleet), pricing, supplier relationships, and any other information designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would consider confidential by its nature.

1.2 **"Patient Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable patient, including but not limited to names, contact details, medical diagnoses, laboratory results, prescriptions, billing information, and any data accessed through the MediFleet Hospital Management System.

1.3 **"MediFleet System"** means the Hospital Management System software used by the Hospital, including all data stored therein.

---

## 2. OBLIGATIONS

2.1 The Staff Member agrees to:

a) **Access only** patient records and hospital information necessary for the performance of their specific role and duties.

b) **Never share** login credentials (username, password) for the MediFleet system or any other hospital system with any other person, including colleagues.

c) **Never access** patient records out of curiosity, personal interest, or for any purpose other than direct patient care or an authorised administrative function.

d) **Never disclose** patient information to any third party (including the patient's own family members) without the patient's explicit consent or a lawful authority (court order, public health obligation).

e) **Immediately report** any suspected data breach, unauthorised access, or security incident to the Data Protection Officer or Hospital Administrator.

f) **Comply** with all provisions of the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and the Kenya Health Act, 2017 as they apply to their role.

g) **Use strong passwords** meeting the Hospital's minimum requirements (8+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, digit, and special character) and change their password immediately upon first login.

h) **Log out** of all systems when leaving a workstation unattended.

i) **Never remove** patient records or hospital data from the hospital premises (physically or electronically) without written authorisation.

---

## 3. SENSITIVE DATA

3.1 The Staff Member acknowledges that the following categories of patient data require heightened protection and must only be accessed by staff with explicit authorisation:

- Mental health records
- Obstetric and reproductive health records
- HIV/AIDS-related records
- Information marked as "sensitive" in the MediFleet system

3.2 Unauthorised access to sensitive patient records constitutes a serious disciplinary offence.

---

## 4. DURATION

4.1 These confidentiality obligations apply:
- **During** employment or contract engagement, and
- **Indefinitely after** the termination of employment for all patient health data.
- For general business confidential information, obligations continue for **5 years** after termination.

---

## 5. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

5.1 Breach of this agreement may result in:
- Disciplinary action up to and including summary dismissal
- Civil liability for damages
- Criminal prosecution under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act, 2018
- Referral to the relevant professional regulatory body
