## What You Can Do

We recommend the following steps to protect yourself:

1. **Be alert for suspicious contact.** If you receive unexpected calls, texts, or emails claiming to be from a healthcare provider, insurance company, or financial institution, be cautious.
2. **Do not share personal details** in response to unsolicited contacts.
3. **Monitor your financial accounts** for any unexpected activity.
4. **Check your medical records** — if you notice any records that do not belong to you, contact us immediately.
5. **Contact us directly** if you have any concerns about how your information may have been used.

- **Correct** any inaccurate information
- **Request erasure** of your personal data (subject to medical records retention requirements)
- **Lodge a complaint** with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)
