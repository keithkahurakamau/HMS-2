## VISITOR GUIDELINES

By signing in, visitors acknowledge and agree to:

1. Abide by the hospital's visiting hours and rules.
2. Not disturb other patients or clinical staff.
3. Maintain silence in clinical areas.
4. Not use mobile phones or cameras in clinical areas without permission.
5. Not bring food or drink into the wards unless approved by nursing staff.
6. Wash or sanitise hands before and after visiting a patient.
7. Vacate the ward immediately when requested by hospital staff.
8. Not access any medical records, equipment, or restricted areas.
