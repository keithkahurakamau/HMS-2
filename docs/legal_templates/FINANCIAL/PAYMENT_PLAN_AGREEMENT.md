## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. **Commitment:** The patient undertakes to make each payment by the due date specified above.

2. **Late Payments:** A payment is considered late if not received within **5 calendar days** of the due date. The hospital will contact the patient upon a missed payment.

3. **Default:** If the patient misses **two or more** consecutive instalments, the full remaining balance becomes immediately due and payable.

4. **Service Continuation:** [HOSPITAL NAME] agrees to continue providing essential medical services to the patient while this agreement is honoured. In the event of default, the hospital reserves the right to require payment of outstanding balances before providing non-emergency services.

5. **Emergency Services:** This agreement does not restrict the patient's access to emergency medical care, which will always be provided regardless of outstanding balances.

6. **Amendment:** Any changes to this schedule must be agreed in writing and signed by both parties.

7. **Governing Law:** This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya.
