| Purpose | Lawful basis under KDPA |
|---|---|
| Create and maintain your portal account | s30 — contract for portal service |
| Authenticate you on sign-in and protect your account from misuse | s30 — contract; legitimate interest in security |
| Send appointment reminders, test-result-available notices, and account-security notices | Consent, which you can withdraw |
| Provide technical support and respond to your queries | Contract; legitimate interest |
| Improve the portal (debug, fix issues, design improvements based on de-identified usage data) | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with our legal obligations (e.g., responding to a court order) | Legal obligation |
| Defend against fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access | Legitimate interest |

We do **not** process your clinical information for any of our own purposes.
Clinical information that flows through our systems is processed only on
behalf of the Hospital under the Hospital's instructions.

## 8. Your rights

You have the same KDPA rights as set out in the Hospital's notice
(Document 03). For matters concerning your portal account specifically:

- **Access:** export your portal-account data via *Settings → Download my data*, or by emailing the DPO.
- **Correction:** edit your details directly in the portal, or contact the DPO.
- **Erasure:** delete your portal account via *Settings → Delete account*, or by emailing the DPO. (Note: clinical records remain at the Hospital.)
- **Restrict / object:** email the DPO.
- **Portability:** export your data via the same route as access.
- **Withdraw consent:** turn off notifications in *Settings → Notifications* at any time.
- **Complaint:** contact the DPO. If unresolved, complain to the ODPC at https://www.odpc.go.ke.

We respond within **thirty (30) days**, extendable by sixty (60) days for
complex requests with reasons given.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your portal account, including:

- Passwords stored as one-way salted hashes; we never see your password.
- HTTPS / TLS encryption for all traffic.
- HttpOnly, Secure session cookies with CSRF protection.
- Rate-limiting and abuse-detection on the sign-in endpoint.
- Internal access to portal records limited to named staff with role-based
  controls and audit logging.

If a breach is likely to result in a high risk to you, we will notify you
without undue delay and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours of
becoming aware, per KDPA s43.

## 11. Children

The portal is intended for users **eighteen (18) years and older**. For
minors who are patients of a Hospital, account access is provided through a
parent or guardian, with consent given per KDPA s33. Hospitals' clinical
workflows for minors are governed by the Hospital's privacy notice and
applicable health law.
